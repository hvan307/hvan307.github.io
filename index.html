Mini Bio

My tech journey started in 2019 when after completing a couple of online courses, I realised that joining the 'secret cool club' of developers might be more achievable than I thought.

With this in mind, in 2020 I decided to enroll to Software Engineering Immersive full-stack development programme at General Assembly, where my journey has truly began. Despite it being the most intense three months of my life, it was the best educational experience to date. Being surrounded by like-minded people, learning heeps, I got to immerse myself in the community both online and offline.

I am very excited to be utilising my knowledge and skills for developing technologies that we use every day and I am looking forward to starting the next chapter as a full-stack developer.
