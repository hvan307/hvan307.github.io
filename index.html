Mini Bio

Programming to me means more than just coding skills; it's also problem-solving and creativity. If you think about it, the possibilities of what can be developed are only limited by the latter. But it's the skills that you have to work on and learn first.

But in the past, I've always been a secret admirer from a far. I was curious and inspired by others' achievements, but personally never had the time and courage to explore it myself. Endless lines of code seemed overwhelming and abstract back then, and I did not know where to begin.

And so, my programming journey didn't really start until 2019. It was when I've committed to learn and completed a couple of online courses. It was love at first sight and I realised that joining the 'secret cool club' of developers might be quite achievable if I put my mind to it.

With this in mind, in 2020 I decided to enrol to Software Engineering Immersive full-stack development programme at General Assembly, where my journey has truly began. Despite it being the most intense three months of my life, it was the best educational experience to date. Being surrounded by like-minded people, learning heaps, I got to immerse myself in the community both online and offline.

I am very excited to be utilising my knowledge and skills for developing technologies that we use every day and I am looking forward to starting the next chapter as a full-stack developer.
